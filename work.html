Research

I am an Applied Statistician and Data Scientist. As a statistician, I have had the good fortune of working on data from many different domains — publishing peer-reviewed work in Genetics, Clinical Trials, Medicine, Marketing, Computer Science, and Geology.

I am currently working as a Principal Research Scientist at Adobe Research in San Jose, CA, where I lead research on statistical methods for digital marketing, online experimentation, causal inference, and AI agents for enterprise analytics.

Research Interests

  • Anytime-valid inference and sequential experimentation
  • Causal inference in digital marketing contexts
  • Bayesian methods for marketing attribution
  • Non-human traffic and bot detection
  • AI agents for data insights and audience analytics
  • Privacy-preserving experimentation
