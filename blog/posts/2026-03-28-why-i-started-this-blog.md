I felt like writing a blog. That's really the whole story.

There are a lot of things I find interesting and want to think through more carefully — some technical, some not. Research topics I'm working on, books I've been reading, music I keep coming back to, the occasional data analysis of a question that's been nagging at me. A blog seemed like a good place for all of it.

No fixed theme. No posting schedule. Just things worth writing down.
